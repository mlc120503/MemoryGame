歡迎來到山並先生的小空間

Instructions:
The game starts with all the cards face down and player have to turn over three cards each round.
If the three cards have no the same picture, then they turn the cards face down again.
Each round you will have 1.5 minutes to finish the matching.

You will score 15 points after all card is matched and lose 1 point each wrong matching.

The number of cards can be set to any multiple of 3.
